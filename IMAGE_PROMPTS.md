# Eventopic — AI Image Generation Prompts

This document contains the fully constructed, copy-pasteable prompts for all your Eventopic website assets, pre-formatted with the **Global Art Direction, Color Palette (royal purple/violet & lavender), Style Suffix, Negative Suffix, and Aspect Ratios (`--ar`)** optimized for Midjourney or other generators (DALL·E 3, Flux, etc.).

---

## Global Directives Summary
- **Look:** Premium, editorial, cinematic Dubai luxury events.
- **Palette:** Bright and airy, royal purple/violet accent, lavender tones, clean white, gold lighting.
- **Composition:** Faces slightly turned or mid-action (less stock-like), negative space for text overlay where noted.
- **Quality:** JPG ~80% for photos (keep site fast), PNG for `#10` (OG image) and `#11` (pattern).
